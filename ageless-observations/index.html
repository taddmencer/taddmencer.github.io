Ageless Observations

I read this email from my mother and thought it was be fun to pass along. You may as well know, I'm becoming 'anti-government' lately after reading and looking and seeing how the big dogs are rolling. There is becoming less and less distinction between Republicans and Democrats. I hope a third party can win the election some day. It may make a huge difference. I'm still for Chuck Baldwin.

Enjoy!
