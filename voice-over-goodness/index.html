Voice Over Goodness

My good friend Henrik Ryosa has started a production company called VoxHouse Studio with another gent out in Sweden. They have some amazing things planned for the near future, one of them is an anime called 'BRUTE'. I can't tell you much about the show, but I'm pretty excited about it. He's using a lot of techniques to really bring out a cool, dirty and dark feel.

One of the reasons, other than it being awesome, that I'm promoting it is I just did a voice demo for a character in the show and sent it to him in hopes that I may have a part. You know, ride his train to fame and all. Well, happily I DID get a part - not the one I tried for but this one sounds like it would be more fun. I'm the silly comedy relief guy. I'm excited. Time to dig out my better recording gear and keep my voice hydrated!

Here is a teaser video for the show. I'm pretty stoked about it.
