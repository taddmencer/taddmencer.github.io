5 Original Soundtracks to Inspire

It always amazes me how music can inspire you. I can’t count how many times a song makes me excited or drives my imagination. I’ve listened to one song dozens of time to experience the feeling over and over again.

Here is a collection of five albums that I’ve personally felt gives my creativity that boost when writing or designing things

1. Lord of the Rings by Howard Shore

The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring: Howard Shore, Enya: Music
The Lord Of The Rings: The Two Towers: Howard Shore, Isabel Bayrakdarian (soprano): Music
The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King: Howard Shore, Annie Lennox: Music

 

2. How to Train Your Dragon by John Powell

How To Train Your Dragon: John Powell: Music

3. Pan's Labyrinth by Javier Navarrete

Pan's Labyrinth: Javier Navarrete: Music

4. Inception by Howard Shore

Inception: Hans Zimmer: Music

5. Pirates Of The Caribbean by Klaus Badelt & Hans Zimmer

Pirates Of The Caribbean: The Curse Of The Black Pearl: Klaus Badelt, Klaus Badelt: Music
Pirates Of The Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest: Hans Zimmer: Music
Pirates Of The Caribbean: At World's End: Hans Zimmer
Pirates Of The Caribbean: On Stranger Tides: Hans Zimmer: Music
