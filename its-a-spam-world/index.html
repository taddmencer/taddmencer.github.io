It's a spam world

I've been getting an excessive amount of spam lately. It can be very amusing, especially since a quart of them are from spam bot programs that are like "Yeah, you want to get tons of traffic? Then use (Spam program name withheld) because it can bypass even the toughest of spam blockers!" But guess where I find all this crap? In the blocked bin with all the porn crap .. thank you Akismet. You are my hero.

But I'm wondering how much I'd get in a week. So, from today until next week I'll let it ride and not clear all the held spam BS and then let you know next Friday how it goes. Should be fun.

Akismet - don't leave home without it.
