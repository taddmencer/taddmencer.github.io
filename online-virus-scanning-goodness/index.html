Online Virus Scanning goodness

One of the employees here at the office was bringing a hard drive full of music and whatnots from home. Little did this employee know, but it was chock full of virus baddies which was causing that employee's computer to fail miserably.

Pete, our office IT guru, set upon the external hard drive an online virus scanning service by Trend Micro. I wasn't really aware that you could do this, for free even, but yes indeed you can.

http://housecall.trendmicro.com/

It's called 'Housecall' and can easily scan your whole system or certain folders that may be suspect.

All over the Internet without having to download anything.

Take note you have to have Java enabled on your browser.
